Destinys Child Wiki Page

BIO

Destinys Child was a R&B girl group that came out of Texas in 1990. The groups best-known line up included Beyonce Knowles, Kelly Rowland, and Michelle Williams, but prior to 2001 the group was also accompanied by 3 other members, Latavia Roberson, LeToya Luckett, & Farrah Franklin. They have sold over 60 million records worldwide and was ranked one of the greatest musical trios of all time. The group has been nominated for 14 Grammy awards as well as featured in a number of films and TV shows. The group split up in 2006 when they ultimately decided to pursue their solo careers in both music and acting.
